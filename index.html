Bombs Away

Introduction

The objective of the game is to defend your city buildings from the bombing raid of the overflying aircraft.

To meet this objective you have an anti-aircraft gun situated at the base of the display. You can move the gun left or right by touch dragging it. The gun fires when you click the red button on the gun.

You score points for each bomber hit by your gun shells. You lose points for every building bombed.

On you go captain!
